About Jurlina Contractors

At Jurlina Contractors, Nick brings over 30 years of expertise in land clearing, earthmoving, and excavation throughout Auckland and the surrounding areas. With a keen eye for perfection, he leads a small, dedicated team that combines top-quality machinery with skilled operators to provide precise, reliable, and safe site preparation for residential, commercial, and rural projects.

Nick has successfully managed numerous tractor mulching and land clearing contracts across Auckland.

He is fully licensed and insured, committed to upholding the highest standards of safety and environmental responsibility. Whether you require site clearing, mulching, or driveway construction, he is ready to deliver your project on time and within budget.
